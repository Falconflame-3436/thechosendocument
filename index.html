Selenite Is Shutting Down
Selenite will officially close on Tuesday, May 5th at 8PM EST. After years of running the site, Skylar is stepping away due to harassment and DMCA takedowns targeting the site. They have a message they'd like you to read.

A Message from Skylar

I wish I did not have to make this announcement, but Selenite will officially be shutting down on Tuesday, May 5th, at 8PM EST.

To the best of my knowledge, I have been a victim of harassment due to blatantly false allegations against my name. They've contacted companies to get them to send DMCA takedown requests and cease and desists to Selenite. As far as I know, this has resulted in a cease and desist from IXL (if you have noticed the name and icon change on the website, this is why) and now DMCAs from Nintendo for every Nintendo game I have on the site, with more potentially coming that I don't even know about.

As of writing this, I have an AP exam in 2 days. I have friends, family, and life which has taken my time. I do not have the time, nor the energy, to deal with stuff like this.

I have been running some form of an unblocked games website since 2022, and in that time, I have made sure it is as high quality as I can make it with as little ads as possible. I've talked with many other site owners, and not only have I received help from them, but I helped them as much as I can. I am aware I have made mistakes, however I have tried my best to grow and help others as much as I can in this space.

I love Selenite. It has been a passion project for years. When I started this site, I didn't know anything. I barely had any experience, but thanks to you guys, I have been able to grow this site and learn so many skills I don't think I could've otherwise. I love it so much that I feel physically ill writing this message. But the community is awful. There is always drama between sites, and more and more things that people are arguing about. Despite this, I have always tried my best to stay neutral and follow my morals. I know I'm not some saint, I've made mistakes, but I've grown, and I try to continue to grow.

One day, Selenite may come back in some capacity. But the chapter of me owning Selenite is over.

Thank you for the past 4 years of my life.

— Skylar
